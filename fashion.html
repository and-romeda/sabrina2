Sabrina Carpenter's Fashion

During Sabrina’s “Short N Sweet Tour” she rocks custom looks from designers known as Victoria's Secret, Patou, and Ludovic de Saint Sernin.

Act I: Custom Victoria’s Secret Corset Bodysuit

During Act I of her performance, she wears a custom Victoria's Secret corset body suit and a matching babydoll dress. She has many colors of this bodysuit/dress combo ranging from baby blue and pink to bright red and blue and many other variations of colors. This act’s outfit is paired with a pair of sheer tights that have different lyrics on her thigh, such as “Taste Me” from her song “Taste.”

Act II: Custom Patou Black Lace Capri Catsuit

During Act II of her show, she debuts a custom Patou black lace capri catsuit. Each bodysuit has a different-colored bow on the front, which matches the color of the feather boa she wears for her song “Feather.” The colors also range from light pastels of pink and yellow to bold, bold colors like blue and burgundy.

Act III: Custom Ludovic de Saint Sernin Two-Piece

In Act III she comes out in a series of custom Ludovic de Saint Sernin two-piece tops with skirts embellished with Swarovski crystals and a matching custom pair of crystal-encrusted platform Naked Wolfe boots featuring side cutout hearts. These custom outfits come in a range of colors, such as red. Black, pastel blue, pink, and many more.

Sabrina's Signature Style

Off-stage, Sabrina is known to wear very girly outfits that range in color and design. They are sometimes seen as very fashionable or just a comfy everyday outfit outfit.
