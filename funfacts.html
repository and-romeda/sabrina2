Sabrina Carpenter's Fun Facts

1. Sabrina's Musical Talents

Sabrina is a multi-talented musician who plays the guitar, piano, and ukulele. Her musical skills are a huge part of her identity, and she incorporates them into her performances and songwriting.

2. Fluent in French

Aside from her music, Sabrina is also fluent in French, which she occasionally shows off in interviews and during her performances.

3. From YouTube Star to Superstar

At just 10 years old, Sabrina started her career as a YouTube star, showcasing her singing and acting abilities. Her online presence helped launch her career, and she soon transitioned into acting and music.

4. Disney Star: Maya in Girl Meets World

Sabrina also became a beloved Disney star, playing Maya Hart on the show "Girl Meets World". She also voiced Princess Vivian in "Sofia the First".

5. A Talented Baker and Cook

When she's not on stage or filming, Sabrina enjoys spending time in the kitchen. She's a talented baker and cook, often sharing her delicious recipes with fans.

6. Painting as Therapy

Sabrina also uses painting as a form of therapy, finding peace and creative expression in the process.
